## What is it?

This is a premium digital e-commerce website focused on **computer embroidery on fashion clothing**. Customers can browse a curated collection of embroidery designs, choose a cloth type (such as cotton, silk, linen, etc.), select a color, and place a custom order that is embroidered and delivered to them. Along with custom embroidery, the website also sells ready-made fashion products like dresses, saree, and other apparel — but the main identity of the brand is **custom embroidered fashion**.

## Who is it for?

This website is for:

- Fashion-conscious customers who want **unique, personalized clothing**
- Boutique shoppers and designers looking for **custom embroidery**
- People buying **ethnic wear, festive wear, and premium everyday fashion**
- Young adults, brides, and professionals who value **craftsmanship and premium feel**

## What's the goal?

The main goal is to:

- Let users **discover embroidery designs**
- **Customize** them by selecting cloth type and color
- **Place an order** for a made-to-order embroidered product
Secondary goals include selling ready-made dresses, sarees, and other fashion products while building a **luxury embroidery brand**.
